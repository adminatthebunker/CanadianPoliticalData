# Contact

| Reason | Best route |
| --- | --- |
| Data correction (one record) | [Submit a correction](../politicians/corrections.md) |
| Bug or site issue | [admin@thebunkerops.ca](mailto:admin@thebunkerops.ca) |
| Coverage request (a jurisdiction we should add) | [admin@thebunkerops.ca](mailto:admin@thebunkerops.ca) |
| Press / interview | [admin@thebunkerops.ca](mailto:admin@thebunkerops.ca) |
| Partnership / institutional access | [admin@thebunkerops.ca](mailto:admin@thebunkerops.ca) |
| Account help | [admin@thebunkerops.ca](mailto:admin@thebunkerops.ca) (from the email on the account) |
| Refund request | [admin@thebunkerops.ca](mailto:admin@thebunkerops.ca) |

## What to include

- **For bugs:** the URL of the page, what you did, what you expected, what
  happened. Screenshots help.
- **For coverage requests:** the jurisdiction, a link to the upstream
  publisher's data (RSS, JSON, HTML), and what you'd most want to be
  able to search for.
- **For partnerships:** a paragraph on what you're trying to do and what
  shape of access would help.

## Response times

- **Bugs and account issues:** usually within a couple of business days.
- **Coverage requests and partnership conversations:** within a week or
  two — we read everything but ingestion work is queued.
- **Press inquiries on deadline:** flag the deadline in your subject
  line ("DEADLINE: Friday 5pm ET") and we'll prioritize.

## Not the right place for

- **Questions for the politicians themselves.** We don't relay messages.
  Their constituency offices (linked from their profile page where
  available) are the right channel.
- **Political opinions.** We don't moderate political debate via email.
- **Public-records requests aimed at a legislature.** Each legislature
  has its own access-to-information process.
