# Buying credits

Credits are bought in **packs**. The current pack lineup and prices are
shown on the [credits page](https://canadianpoliticaldata.org/credits) — we
don't reproduce them in docs because they change. Larger packs cost less
per credit.

## Payment

Payment is processed by **Stripe**. We don't see your card number — only a
Stripe-issued token and the order metadata. Your card details are never
stored on our servers.

Accepted methods (varies by region):

- Major credit and debit cards
- Apple Pay / Google Pay (in supporting browsers)

## Canadian sales tax

If Canadian sales tax (GST / HST / PST / QST) applies, it's calculated at
checkout based on the billing address you provide and shown clearly before
you confirm the purchase. The breakdown is preserved on your receipt.

If you're a registered Canadian business and want to enter your GST / HST
number to receive a tax-compliant invoice, the checkout form has a field
for it.

## Receipts

After purchase you'll get an email receipt from Stripe. The credits land
in your balance immediately on successful payment — usually within a few
seconds.

You can see your full purchase history on your
[account page](https://canadianpoliticaldata.org/account), with links to
each receipt.

## Refunds

We offer refunds:

- **Within 7 days of purchase** for *unspent* credits, no questions asked.
  Email [admin@thebunkerops.ca](mailto:admin@thebunkerops.ca) from the
  account email.
- **Anytime** if a report failed due to a bug on our side — that report's
  cost is automatically refunded to your balance, no manual request
  needed.

Spent credits — credits used to generate a report that was delivered as
expected — are not refundable. The compute cost has already been incurred.

## Earning credits

Two non-purchase ways to get credits:

1.  **Submit accepted corrections.** Each accepted correction earns
    credits (typically 10). See [Submitting corrections](../politicians/corrections.md).
2.  **Admin grants.** Occasionally we grant credits to early users, beta
    testers, journalists, and educators. If you fit one of those and
    haven't been credited, [contact us](../about/contact.md).

## Credits don't expire

A credit you buy today is the same credit if you spend it three years from
now. We don't apply expiry dates and won't introduce them silently.

## Why we don't take crypto

We considered it. The combination of:

- Volatile price exposure (we'd have to mark to market constantly)
- Higher fraud risk (irreversible payments make abuse harder to manage)
- Negligible user demand from our actual audience

…means it doesn't make sense for this product. If that changes we'll
revisit.

## Privacy of purchase data

We store the bare minimum: the Stripe customer ID, the pack purchased,
the amount paid (gross), and the timestamp. We do **not** see or store
your card number, billing address, or any of the data Stripe holds on
your behalf.

Your purchase history is visible only to you and to the operators of the
site (for support and tax-reporting purposes). It is not shared with any
third party for marketing.
